Personality

My story

Hello! My name is Harry Cooper, my creative nickname is h/cooper. Born and raised in Amsterdam, Netherlands , among the mountains of the North Caucasus. Nowdays I live and work in Usa. I started photographing at 16. And I can not imagine my life without it.

Photo art is an immense space for thoughts and feelings, an endless search for beauty in everything! I specialize in individual portrait photoshoots, fashion photoshoots, also love story and wedding photography. Collaborated with many local publications. Available for cooperation. I am extremely passionate about what I do, and I hope you can see this from viewing my images. I love emotive, natural photography and want you to feel as though you could be in a magazine when looking at your images and give you something a little bit more.

I capture natural but refined moments in a relaxed and stylish way, always looking for the perfect light. Photography is art and that is what I want to create for you. If you care about artistic imagery, think my style suits you perfectly and you feel that we would get along - get in touch, and we can create something special.

I've been collecting my work on this site. Wanna see?
